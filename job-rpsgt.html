Job Summary

The Polysomnographic Technologist is a critical member of our healthcare team, responsible for conducting comprehensive sleep studies and assisting in the diagnosis and treatment of sleep disorders. This role requires technical proficiency, a deep understanding of sleep medicine, and a compassionate, child-friendly approach.

Key Responsibilities

Clinical Duties

  • Conduct overnight and daytime sleep studies (polysomnography) on pediatric and adult patients
  • Prepare patients and families for studies, fit monitoring equipment, and ensure maximum comfort
  • Perform CPAP/BiPAP titration studies and real-time mask adjustments
  • Monitor and document physiological data throughout the night
  • Collect, analyze, and score sleep study data according to AASM standards
  • Troubleshoot equipment and maintain proper calibration
  • Educate families on home equipment use when needed

Administrative & Collaborative Duties

  • Maintain accurate patient records and study documentation
  • Collaborate with sleep physicians to develop individualized treatment plans
  • Stay current with advancements in pediatric sleep medicine
  • Ensure full compliance with clinic policies and HIPAA

Qualifications & Skills

  • Certification by the Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists (RPSGT) or equivalent required
  • Experience with pediatric sleep studies strongly preferred
  • Strong knowledge of sleep physiology and medical terminology
  • Proficiency with PSG equipment, CPAP/BiPAP, and EMR systems
  • Excellent communication and patient/family interaction skills
  • Detail-oriented with strong multitasking abilities
