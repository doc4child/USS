Dr. Minal Patel, M.D.

Minal Patel, M.D., is a board-certified sleep medicine specialist and pediatrician who provides comprehensive sleep evaluations and care for patients of all ages.

  • Training: Pediatric residency and sleep medicine fellowship — University of Mississippi Medical Center, Jackson.
  • Academic leadership: Former Program Director, Sleep Medicine Fellowship & Assistant Professor of Sleep Medicine, University of Mississippi Medical Center.
  • Local practice: Founder — Universal Sleep Solutions; also affiliated with Teddy Bear Pediatrics and Sleep Medicine.
  • Certifications & services: Board-certified in sleep medicine, Inspire-certified, and operating a sleep lab for diagnostic studies across all ages.

Universal Sleep Solutions will offer diagnostic sleep studies, PAP evaluation and titration, pediatric sleep consultations, and adult sleep medicine services. Our team emphasizes evidence-based care, family-centered communication, and close coordination with referring providers.
